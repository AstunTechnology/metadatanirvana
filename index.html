Answer: By recording the provenance, scope, quality and history of data, maintaining it's integrity

New and lesser-known Geonetwork features

Geonetwork is the leading open source, standards-compliant metadata catalog, used globally for national and international spatial data infrastructures

The project is run by a Project Steering Committee, comprising lead developers and users from around the world. It's also an Open Source Geospatial Foundation project, which means it has passed a stringent incubation process focusing on code quality and sustainability

Geonetwork allows you to search and view metadata records and explore linked data resources, but the key enhancements we're going to discuss are around simplifying the production of metadata that's not only valid, but also high quality
